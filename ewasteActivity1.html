Reduce Reality Simulator

Every tech decision creates a future — choose wisely.

Purpose of this activity: To help you understand that the most effective way to reduce e-waste is by making smarter consumer choices at the source. By repairing, upgrading, or extending the life of devices instead of replacing them unnecessarily, we can dramatically lower e-waste generation, save energy, and reduce environmental pollution.

This is not a quiz — there are no right or wrong answers in the traditional sense. Instead, it's an interactive simulation that shows the real-world consequences of everyday tech decisions. You'll go through 3 common scenarios and see the impact of each choice immediately.

Choose thoughtfully and see how small decisions add up to make a big difference for the planet.
