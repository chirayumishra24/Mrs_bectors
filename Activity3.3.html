Cremica’s Actual Positioning

Cremica positions itself as “Affordable Luxury” — firmly in the Mid-Premium space with selective Premium / Health extensions.

This sweet spot avoids direct price wars with mass brands (Parle, Britannia economy lines) while offering superior taste and quality perception versus pure economy players.

Primary audience: Urban millennials & young couples + Budget-conscious families seeking occasional indulgence.

This positioning builds loyalty through perceived value and emotional connection rather than lowest price.

Strong brands don’t try to be everything — they choose where to win.
